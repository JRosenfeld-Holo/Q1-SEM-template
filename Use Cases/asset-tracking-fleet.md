## What This Use Case Is

GPS/location tracking of moving assets — vehicles, shipping containers, equipment, bikes, scooters. Real-time or near-real-time positioning with geofencing and route optimization. Cellular leads as the dominant connectivity method for fleet telematics.

---

## Connectivity Profile

| Attribute | Spec |
|---|---|
| **Bandwidth** | Low to medium (GPS pings, periodic telemetry) |
| **Latency** | Moderate (near-real-time acceptable for most; critical for safety/theft alerts) |
| **Uptime** | High (tracking gaps = blind spots = liability) |
| **Data consumption** | Low per device, but massive scale (thousands to millions of assets) |
| **Protocols** | 4G LTE, Cat-M1 |

## Pain Points (Cross-Industry)

### From Hologram's fleet tracking discovery calls (top 5 reasons prospects switch):
1. **Cross-border/international coverage** — #1 driver. Containers and fleets cross borders. "We need one SIM that works in 50+ countries."
2. **Local carrier instability** — Dead zones or frequent outages in critical coverage areas.
3. **Cost** — Paying too much per device. Multiple carrier contracts. Unused SIMs still billing.
4. **Scaling from prototype to production** — Growing from 100 to 10,000 devices. Current plan doesn't scale.
5. **Poor account management** — Large carrier doesn't give them attention.

### Additional triggers:
- New international route expansion
- Regulatory compliance (cold chain continuous monitoring)
- Carrier contract renewal
- M&A / fleet consolidation

## Emerging: Autonomous & Satellite Hybrid

### Autonomous Trucking & Yard Management
- Estimated 80K driver shortfall in the US
- Autonomous trucks can reduce TCO by 30%+
- Autonomous yard trucks growing at **52.7% CAGR** (2022-2030)
- Multi-carrier failover is safety-critical — connectivity drop means vehicle stops

### Satellite + Cellular Hybrid
- LEO satellite modems operating alongside cellular
- Cellular + satellite fallback for outage-proof reliability
- Relevant for fleet operating across urban (dense cellular) and remote (satellite-only) locations
